Careers

Careers

Great opportunities!

At Safeguard on Demand LLC we aim to offer the best private security services in the California. In order to do so, we are always interested in high quality applicants looking to build a solid and rewarding career as a security officer. We have part-time, full-time, and commission-based positions available, and offer competitive compensation, a supportive environment, and ongoing training to those who qualify.

Only the best!

We require all candidates to complete a strict hiring process that involves a preliminary application; a series of interviews; verification of previous employment; personal reference checks; drug testing; and a full background check, including criminal and civil court history, workers compensation report, and motor vehicle report. If you meet our evaluation criteria, you'll then work to obtain a Security Guard Card from the Bureau of Security and Investigative Services (BSIS).

100% commitment

This means that you must complete and pass a rigorous training process that includes learning about a number of subjects: Power to Arrest, Terrorism and WMD Awareness, Communication, Public Relations, Patrol, General Duties, Emergency Situations, Observe & Report, Post Orders, Fire Watch, Field Training, and more.

These are some of the services we offer in California.

24/7 Dispatch

Dispatchers work around the clock monitoring and maintaining client assets. Monitoring gives a security system its true value. In case of an in-home emergency, one touch of the burglary, fire or medical emergency button on your keypad will get you a response from a live Safeguard on Demand operator. You won't have to worry about dialing 911. Our monitoring center plays a critical role even in moments when nobody is home. If an intrusion is detected or if an abnormal amount of smoke is detected, which may signal a fire, our monitoring center goes into action on your behalf.

Vehicle Patrol

Vehicle Officers patrol properties within their respected areas. Periodic Patrol inspections to a certain location, on nightly basis as frequent as the clients deem necessary to fit their needs. Patrol inspections vary in length but expected to be anywhere between 10-15 minutes per inspection. Inspections are in a 24-hour period, seven days per week and may be day or night, as you direct. Duties are agreed upon prior to commencement of service, which includes, locking up the facilities, unlocks, parking enforcement, crime deterrent, etc.

Corporate Security

Safeguard on Demand provides businesses, Law firms, and government agencies with an "on-call" security team. The purpose of any security program is to protect life and property through identification of threats to security and safety and through prevention of the problems resulting from these threats. A building is made to be used and the activities that go on inside it represent the livelihoods of a lot of people. Along with providing uniformed security personnel, Safeguard on Demand Security provides an assortment of full-service security programs.

Event Security

Crowd management, and guest services for major concerts, sporting events, festivals, conventions, award shows, private parties and functions. Safeguard on Demand identifies and effectively mitigates or manages, at an early stage, any developments that may threaten the resilience and continued survival of a corporation. Construction Site Security

the need for on-site security is especially prudent in and around construction sites regardless of the size of the job. Laying a good foundation for security is a prerequisite for success just like laying a good foundation for a building. Every construction site has sub-contractors from many disciplines, which means much valuable equipment is available to tempt the experienced and amateur criminal alike. Customarily, each sub-contractor is accountable for his own equipment, but that doesn’t always relieve the general contractor from responsibility and sometimes penalty. Properly planned and implemented security can deter most internal and external thieves.

Alarm Response

Safeguard on Demand professionals have an impressive response team for triggered alarms. officers are equipped to handle situations for all levels. your safety is our concern. Safeguard on Demand can serve as your security-system local contact if your alarm system is activated. Notify your alarm monitoring company to call us, as well as the appropriate local authorities. Safeguard on Demand can respond to any alarm-system activation, no matter who your provider is. Our alarm response officers will be notified to respond to your alarm if in fact we determine that the alarm real as well as the local authorities.
